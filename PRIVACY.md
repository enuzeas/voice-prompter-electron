# Privacy Policy for Focus Prompter

**Effective Date:** 2024-05-21

## 1. Data Collection
Focus Prompter ("the Extension") does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

## 2. Audio Data
- **Voice Recognition**: Voice recognition is processed locally within your browser using the Web Speech API.
- **No Server Transmission**: Audio data is **never** sent to our servers or any third-party servers by this Extension.

## 3. Local Storage
- **User Settings**: Data such as font size, speed, and selected microphone are stored in your local browser storage (`localStorage`).
- **Scripts**: Your prompter scripts are stored locally using `IndexedDB`.
- **Control**: You have full control over this data and can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
- We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support section on the Chrome Web Store.
